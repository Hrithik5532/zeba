About Us

  2. About Us

ZEBA  has gone through many changes since its inception, but in every step of the way we haven’t lost touch with what's important to our customers - Luxury Style and Quality Service.

We initially started with a retail store from Salts Mill, world heritage site in Saltaire offering customer co-ordinated range of home furnishings and handmade floor coverings, bringing designer style and expert knowledge to every home.

ZEBA designs its own collections as well as creates products based on trend specific inputs from its customers. We believe we have a fine tuned capability of interpreting and translating into reality customers brief which makes it unique and truly bespoke.

We would like to welcome you all to our new showroom in Shipley (Bentley House, 202 Market Street, Shipley, West Yorkshire, BD18 2BY) where you can browse through a wide range of designers fabrics, wallpapers, handmade rugs, carpets, wood flooring. curtains, blinds, furniture, accessories and many more products.

Our Contract division caters to hotel industry, restaurants, residential homes, luxury homes and corporate offices.
We specialise in bespoke services such as made to order rugs, carpets, curtains, blinds and re-upholstery.
